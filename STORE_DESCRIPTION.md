Chrome Recorder

Chrome Recorder is a versatile in-browser audio workstation designed to make capturing, trimming, and managing web audio a seamless experience.

** For returning users **
Update your extension today to explore our new customizable interface and improved processing engine!

** Support **
Have a question or a feature request? Please feel free to reach out—we’re always looking for ways to improve your recording experience.


Top features:
* Ultra-high resolution capture with sample rates reaching 192kHz
* Multi-format export options including WAV, MP3, OGG, and WebM
* Personalize your workspace with 5 vibrant themes: Light, Dark, Midnight, Forest, and Rainbow
* Toggle between professional stereo and space-efficient mono modes
* Precision editing with interactive waveforms, non-destructive trimming, and vertical zoom
* Intelligent session naming that syncs with your active browser tab
* Deep history library powered by IndexedDB for seamless large-file management
* Studio-grade tools like automatic peak normalization and variable bit-depth (up to 32-bit)
